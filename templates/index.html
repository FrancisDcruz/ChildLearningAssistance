Who are SL-Children?

Neurological disorders of children, such as Autism, take a long time to diagnose and to prepare developmental plan for education and support. Children with such special needs require continuous monitoring, individual development plan and supervision managed by different authorities. Their needs can be well supported by using intelligent and usable technologies. The researchers present a model of multimodal interface using intelligent algorithm, wireless sensors for diagnosis, education and support of the children.
